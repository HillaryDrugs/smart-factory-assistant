Fault Case 001 - Overheating
Symptoms: Temperature rising above 85°C, occasional thermal trips.
Root cause: Clogged heat exchanger and weak cooling fan.
Recommended action: Clean heat exchanger fins, test fan RPM under load,
replace fan if airflow is below specification.

Fault Case 002 - Excessive Vibration
Symptoms: Vibration above 5 mm/s, audible humming, premature bearing wear.
Root cause: Shaft misalignment combined with imbalanced rotor.
Recommended action: Perform laser alignment, balance the rotor, and inspect
bearing housing for cracks.

Fault Case 003 - Pressure Loss
Symptoms: System pressure drops below 2 bar during operation.
Root cause: Worn valve seal causing internal leakage.
Recommended action: Replace valve seal, inspect upstream filter, and verify
pressure regulator setpoint.

Fault Case 004 - RPM Instability
Symptoms: Motor RPM oscillates outside the ±5% tolerance band.
Root cause: Faulty encoder feedback or poorly tuned controller.
Recommended action: Inspect encoder cable, replace if damaged, then re-tune
PID gains starting from manufacturer defaults.

Fault Case 005 - Combined Temperature + Vibration
Symptoms: Both temperature and vibration exceed normal ranges simultaneously.
Root cause: Bearing failure causing friction heat and mechanical imbalance.
Recommended action: Stop the machine immediately. Replace bearing,
flush lubrication system, and re-align shaft before restart.

Fault Case 006 - Pressure + Temperature
Symptoms: Pressure spikes accompanied by temperature creep.
Root cause: Partial blockage in cooling line restricts flow.
Recommended action: Flush cooling circuit, replace filter cartridges,
and verify pump output.
